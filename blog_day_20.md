### 1. The Sleep Company: SmartGrid Tech
The "Grid" is a hyper-elastic polymer structure.
*   **The Tech:** It’s 80% air. This means it doesn't "trap" heat like solid foam does.
*   **The Support:** The walls of the grid buckle under heavy pressure (hips) but stay upright under light pressure (waist).
*   **Best For:** Hot sleepers, people with Sciatica, and those who want a "responsive" feel that isn't as bouncy as a spring.

### 2. Wakefit: Zoned Memory Foam
Wakefit uses high-density memory foam with targeted contouring.
*   **The Tech:** Their Zoned-support layers are designed to allow the shoulders to sink more than the spine.
*   **The Support:** It provides a "hugging" feel. It’s excellent for pressure relief on the joints.
*   **Best For:** Side sleepers, budget-conscious buyers, and those who prefer the classic "quicksand" feel of memory foam.

### Sandeep’s Expert Verdict
*   **Choose The Sleep Company if:** You live in a humid city (Mumbai/Chennai) and find memory foam too hot or too "mushy."
*   **Choose Wakefit if:** You are under 30, have no chronic back issues, and want the best possible sleep for the lowest possible price.
