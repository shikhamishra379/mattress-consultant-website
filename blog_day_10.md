"10 Years Warranty!" is the biggest marketing headline in the mattress industry. But in my 22 years of auditing brands, I have seen thousands of claims rejected. Why? Because the warranty is designed to protect the manufacturer, not the consumer.

### The "1.5-Inch Rule" Trap
Most Indian and Global brands only cover sagging if it is deeper than 1.5 inches. 
*   **The Problem:** A 1-inch sag is enough to ruin your spinal alignment and cause chronic back pain. If your bed sags by 1 inch, you are in pain, but your warranty is invalid.

### The "Stain" Clause
Did you know that a single coffee stain or a small yellow patch can void your entire 10-year warranty? Manufacturers argue that moisture (sweat or spills) degrades the foam, and therefore any sag is your fault.
*   **Solution:** Always use a high-quality, breathable mattress protector from Day 1.

### Prorated vs. Non-Prorated
*   **Non-Prorated:** They replace the mattress for free (minus shipping).
*   **Prorated:** This is the trap. In year 5 of a 10-year warranty, they might only cover 50% of the cost. You end up paying for half a new mattress anyway.

### Sandeep’s Advice: Buy the Product, Not the Promise
A warranty is a legal document, not a quality guarantee. Focus on the **Material Specs** (Latex, Pocket Spring quality, Foam Density) during your purchase. A high-quality mattress shouldn't need a warranty claim in the first place.
