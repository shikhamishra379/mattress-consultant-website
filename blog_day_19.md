### 1. Strip Away the "Marketing Fabric"
Brands often charge a premium for high-GSM (Grams per Square Meter) fabric covers with "Gold Thread" or "Infused Mint." 
*   **The Reality:** You are going to put a mattress protector and a bedsheet over it. The "feel" of the expensive fabric is lost the moment you make the bed.
*   **The Saving:** Focus on the core material (Latex or Spring gauge) and opt for a standard high-quality cover.

### 2. The "NASA Foam" Markup
Memory foam is a 50-year-old technology. Labeling it "NASA-grade" is a justification for a 30% markup.
*   **The Reality:** High-density PU foam (40D+) provides the same support as "Astro-Foam" at a fraction of the cost.
*   **The Saving:** Don't pay for the space-age story; pay for the density (kg/m3).

### 3. Showroom Overheads
Physical stores have rent, electricity, and staff commissions. These are baked into the price of the mattress you see on the floor.
*   **The Reality:** Many D2C brands offer the exact same (or superior) material specs online for 40% less.
*   **The Saving:** Use an independent consultant to verify if an online alternative matches the showroom quality.
