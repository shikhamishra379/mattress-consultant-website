Two giants dominate the traditional Indian mattress showrooms: **Duroflex** and **Sleepwell**. Both have launched "Scientific" ranges—the *Duropedic* and the *Nexa* series. But which one actually delivers on spinal alignment? I’ve performed technical teardowns on both for my elite clients.

### 1. Duroflex Duropedic: The NHA Certified Choice
Duroflex's Duropedic range is the only one in India certified by the National Health Academy.
*   **The Tech:** It uses a 5-Zone Full Prone Support System. This is a firmer setup designed to keep the spine neutral. 
*   **The Feel:** It provides a "Traditional Ortho" feel—stiff but supportive.
*   **Best For:** Patients recovering from Slipped Disc or those who prefer the firm "push" of high-density coir and foam blends.

### 2. Sleepwell Nexa: The Responsive Luxury
The Nexa series is Sleepwell's answer to modern memory foam.
*   **The Tech:** Nexa is a proprietary "smart" foam that contours like memory foam but has the quick recovery of latex. It prevents that "sinking into quicksand" feeling.
*   **The Feel:** Luxurious, plush, and highly responsive. 
*   **Best For:** Luxury residences where comfort is as important as support, and for sleepers who move frequently at night.

### Sandeep’s Comparison Verdict
*   **Choose Duroflex Duropedic if:** You have a medical requirement for a very firm, non-sink surface.
*   **Choose Sleepwell Nexa if:** You want a premium, hotel-like feel without sacrificing the ease of turning over.
