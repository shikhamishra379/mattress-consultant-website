### What You Are Paying For in a ₹1L+ Mattress
1. **Material Purity:** Luxury beds often use **100% Natural Talalay Latex** or organic wool. These materials stay cool for 15+ years, whereas cheap foam begins to degrade (and trap heat) in just 3 years.
2. **Support Architecture:** Premium mattresses aren't just "blocks of foam." They feature **Zoned Support Systems**. This means the mattress is firmer under your lower back and softer under your shoulders—a critical requirement for Sciatica and Slipped Disc recovery.
3. **Coil-on-Coil Technology:** Many luxury hybrids feature thousands of micro-coils that provide a level of contouring that standard pocket springs cannot match.

### The Marketing "Fluff" to Avoid
Be wary of "Luxury" brands that boast about "Diamond Dust Infusions" or "Gold-Thread Covers." These do **nothing** for your spinal alignment. They are "Vanity Specs" designed to justify a high price.

### Sandeep’s Luxury Verdict
A high-ticket mattress is worth the investment **ONLY IF** the material science matches your body weight (BMI). If you are 95kg+, a luxury hybrid with reinforced coils is a necessity, not a luxury. If you are 55kg, you can achieve elite sleep on a much cheaper, high-quality latex mattress.
