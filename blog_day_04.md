When he showed me his current setup, the problem was obvious. He was using a very soft memory foam mattress that allowed his hips to sink deep, but his pillow was firm and high.
*   **The Result:** His spine was bent like a bow. No matter how "medical" the pillow was, his neck was being pulled out of alignment by his sagging hips.

### The 15-Minute Audit Solution
